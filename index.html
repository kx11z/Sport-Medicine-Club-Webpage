About Our Club

The Sports Medicine Club explores the science behind athletic performance, injury prevention, and recovery. Members learn about biomechanics, rehabilitation, nutrition, and medical technologies used to keep athletes healthy.

Through workshops, interactive lessons, and research discussions, we aim to inspire students interested in medicine, physiology, sports science, and healthcare careers.
