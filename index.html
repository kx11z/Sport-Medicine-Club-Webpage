About Our Club

The Sports Medicine Club explores the science behind athletic performance, injury prevention, and recovery. Members learn about biomechanics, physical therapy, rehabilitation, and sports technology.

Through workshops, collaborations with athletes, and guest speakers, our goal is to help students understand how medicine and engineering can work together to improve athlete health.
