About Our Club

What is Sports Medicine?

A branch of medicine focused on sports injuries, including prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation.

Our Purpose

  • Educate about the broad field of sports medicine
  • Help members maintain personal health
  • Provide opportunities to expand knowledge and understanding
